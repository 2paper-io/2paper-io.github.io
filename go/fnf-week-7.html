Having Trouble Playing Games? We're Here to Help!

Having trouble getting your game on? Don't fret! We understand the frustration of encountering issues while trying to enjoy our games. Take a screenshot of the exact problem you're facing on your computer. This will give our support team a clear view of the issue and allow them to diagnose it more effectively. 2. Send Us an Email: Once you have your screenshot, send it to our email address: 2paper-iocontact@gmail.com. Our dedicated support staff will be waiting to receive your email and assist you.
